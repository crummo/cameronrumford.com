About

Cameron Rumford is a Los Angeles based editor, proficient in Adobe Premiere, AVID, and DaVinci Resolve.

With over 18 years in post-production, Cameron brings a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for the art of crafting narratives — shaping structure, fine-tuning dialogue, and perfecting pacing. Additional skills include color grading and audio mixing. Career highlights include working as an assistant editor on the 2011 Oscar-winning Best Documentary Undefeated.

For work inquiries: camrumford@gmail.com
